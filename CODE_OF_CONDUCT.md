# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want vibe-hardware to be a friendly, welcoming place — especially for people new
to hardware. Everyone is welcome regardless of experience level or background.

## Be excellent

- Be kind, patient, and constructive. "How do I even start a PCB?" is a great question.
- Critique ideas and designs, not people.
- Assume good faith; help others learn.

## Not OK

Harassment, insults, discrimination, or sharing others' private information. Don't be
a jerk.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code, and may
warn or ban repeat offenders. Report issues to the maintainer
([@luckiday](https://github.com/luckiday)).

This is a condensed adaptation of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
